Hey, I'm Dr. Megan Perram.

// Exploring digital narrative frontiers //

This space showcases my academic work and projects — navigate the tabs to explore.

I'm an Assistant Professor of English in Digital Literature and New Media Studies in the Department of English and Creative Writing at Campion College, University of Regina. My research focuses on illness narrative authorship and storytelling for marginalized and pathologized identities through novel technology including generative AI, hypertext, virtual reality, social media apps, and video games.

I was a former Senior Policy Advisor with the Alberta government advising on mandatory drug addiction treatment legislation. A hardcore Taylor Swift fan, I also dabble in research within Swiftian Studies.

My first book, Hyperandrogenism and the Marginalization of Pathologized Bodies: Exploring Therapeutic Storytelling in Hypertext Illness Narratives, is published with Routledge (2025).
